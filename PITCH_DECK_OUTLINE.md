## PRESENTATION TIPS

### Timing:
- **10-12 minutes**: Core presentation
- **3-5 minutes**: Q&A
- Practice to stay within time limit

### Design Principles:
- **Visual over text**: Use images, charts, diagrams
- **One idea per slide**: Keep it simple
- **Consistent branding**: TrueHome colors, fonts, style
- **High-quality visuals**: Professional screenshots, charts

### Delivery Tips:
- **Start strong**: Problem slide should hook them
- **Tell a story**: Connect slides with narrative
- **Show passion**: Believe in what you're building
- **Be prepared**: Know your numbers, anticipate questions
- **Practice**: Rehearse multiple times

### Key Numbers to Memorize:
- Market size: $200B+
- Report price: $49.99
- Target customers: 2M+ home buyers/year
- Year 3 revenue target: $10M ARR
- Gross margin: 70%+
- CAC target: <$50
- LTV target: $200+

### Common Questions to Prepare For:
1. "What's your unfair advantage?"
2. "How do you acquire customers cost-effectively?"
3. "What prevents Zillow/Redfin from building this?"
4. "How do you ensure data accuracy?"
5. "What's your path to profitability?"
6. "Who are your first customers?"
7. "What's your customer acquisition strategy?"
8. "How do you scale data collection?"
