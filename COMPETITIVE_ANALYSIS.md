# TrueHome vs. RealReports.ai - Competitive Analysis

## Executive Summary

**RealReports.ai** is an established, nationwide AI-powered property intelligence platform targeting real estate professionals (agents, MLSs). **TrueHome** is a focused, regional platform targeting both consumers (buyers) and professionals, with a "Carfax for homes" approach emphasizing comprehensive property history.

---

## Platform Overview

### RealReports.ai
- **Target Market**: Real estate agents, MLSs, real estate professionals
- **Coverage**: Nationwide (all 50 states)
- **Business Model**: B2B (MLS partnerships, agent subscriptions)
- **Key Differentiator**: AI-powered analysis (Aiden AI assistant), 60+ data providers
- **Founded**: Established player with multiple partnerships

### TrueHome
- **Target Market**: Home buyers, realtors, property investors
- **Coverage**: Regional focus (starting with Collin County, Texas)
- **Business Model**: B2C + B2B (per-report pricing, credit packs)
- **Key Differentiator**: Deep regional history, certified contractor network, consumer-friendly
- **Status**: Early stage, building comprehensive regional database

## Conclusion

**RealReports.ai** is the established leader in AI-powered property intelligence for professionals, with nationwide coverage and advanced AI features.

**TrueHome** has a unique opportunity to differentiate through:
1. **Consumer focus** (vs. agent-only)
2. **Certified contractor network** (unique feature)
3. **Regional depth** (comprehensive local history)
4. **Transparent pricing** (accessible to consumers)

**Recommendation**: TrueHome should:
- **Short-term**: Perfect Collin County coverage, build contractor network
- **Medium-term**: Add AI features, expand regionally
- **Long-term**: Consider nationwide expansion, but maintain regional depth advantage

**Key Differentiator**: TrueHome's consumer-friendly approach and certified contractor network provide unique value that RealReports.ai doesn't offer, making TrueHome complementary rather than directly competitive in the consumer market.
