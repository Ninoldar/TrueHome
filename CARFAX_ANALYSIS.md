# Carfax Feature Analysis for TrueHome

## Carfax Features for Vehicles → Home Property Equivalents

### ✅ Currently Implemented

1. **Sales History** (Vehicle Registration/Title)
   - ✅ Ownership chain with dates
   - ✅ Sale prices
   - ✅ Buyers/sellers
   - ⚠️ Missing: Title issues (liens, disputes, foreclosures)

2. **Work Records** (Service/Repair Records)
   - ✅ Work type and descriptions
   - ✅ Contractor information
   - ✅ Dates and costs
   - ✅ Permits
   - ⚠️ Missing: Regular maintenance logs (HVAC servicing, inspections)

3. **Insurance Claims** (Accident/Damage History)
   - ✅ Claim types and amounts
   - ✅ Claim dates
   - ✅ Status
   - ✅ Insurance company

4. **Warranties** (Recall Information)
   - ✅ Active warranties
   - ✅ Expiration dates
   - ✅ Coverage details

### ❌ Missing Features (Need to Add)

1. **Title Issues & Liens**
   - Property liens
   - Title disputes
   - Foreclosure history
   - Tax liens
   - HOA liens

2. **Usage History**
   - Rental property history
   - Commercial use
   - Short-term rental (Airbnb)
   - Owner-occupied vs investment

3. **Environmental & Safety**
   - Lead paint disclosure
   - Asbestos presence
   - Radon testing
   - Mold issues
   - Flood zone status
   - Environmental hazards

4. **Maintenance Schedule**
   - Regular HVAC servicing
   - Roof inspections
   - Plumbing checks
   - Electrical inspections
   - Foundation inspections

5. **Property Condition Score**
   - Overall condition rating
   - Age of major systems
   - Estimated remaining life
   - Risk assessment score

6. **Comparable Properties**
   - Similar properties in area
   - Market trends
   - Price per square foot
   - Days on market

7. **Neighborhood Information**
   - Crime statistics
   - School ratings
   - Property value trends
   - Development plans

8. **Visual Timeline**
   - Chronological timeline of events
   - Visual representation
   - Key milestones

9. **Risk Flags**
   - Red flags (like Carfax's damage indicators)
   - Yellow flags (caution items)
   - Green flags (positive indicators)

10. **Report Number & Verification**
    - Unique report number
    - Report generation date
    - Data source verification
    - Report validity period

## Priority Implementation Plan

### Phase 1: Core Enhancements (High Priority)
1. Title issues and liens tracking
2. Usage history (rental/commercial)
3. Property condition score
4. Enhanced risk flags

### Phase 2: Environmental & Safety (Medium Priority)
1. Environmental assessments
2. Safety compliance
3. Hazard disclosures

### Phase 3: Market Intelligence (Lower Priority)
1. Comparable properties
2. Neighborhood trends
3. Market analysis
